Layout Helpers

Conditional Content
.desktop-only
hides elements on ipad, small-tablet and mobile devices
.hide-on-desktop
hides elements on devices with viewport widths larger than 1024 pixels
.ipad-only
only shows elements on devices with viewport widths between 768 and 1024 pixels
.hide-on-ipad
hides elements on devices with viewport widths between 768 and 1024 pixels
.small-tablet-only
only shows elements on devices with viewport widths smaller than 768 pixels
.hide-on-small-tablet
hides elements on devices with viewport widths smaller than 768 pixels
.mobile-only
only shows elements on devices with viewport widths of 480 pixels and below
.hide-on-mobile
hides elements on devices with viewport widths of 480 pixels and below
